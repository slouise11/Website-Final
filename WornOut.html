Worn Out

Worn Out was drawn with pencil on paper by Vincent van Gogh in November 1882 while he was in The Hague, Netherlands. This powerful black-and-white pencil drawing depicts an elderly man sitting hunched over in a chair, his head in his hands, conveying exhaustion and despair.
