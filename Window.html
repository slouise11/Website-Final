Window in the Studio

Window in the Studio was painted with chalk, brush and oil paint and watercolour, on paper by Vincent van Gogh in September - October 1889 while he was at the Saint-Paul-de-Mausole asylum in Saint-RÃ©my-de-Provence, France. This work captures the view from his studio window, offering a glimpse of the outside world through thick, expressive brushstrokes. Using a mix of muted and vibrant tones, Van Gogh conveys both a sense of confinement and longing for nature.
