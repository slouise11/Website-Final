Congregation Leaving the Reformed Church in Nuenen

Congregation Leaving the Reformed Church in Nuenen was painted with oil on canvas by Vincent van Gogh in January - Feburary 1884 while he was living in Nuenen, Netherlands. This somber painting depicts churchgoers exiting the village church where Van Gogh’s father was a pastor. Originally created as a gift for his mother, the work was later altered, with Van Gogh adding figures dressed in mourning attire after his father’s death in Autumn 885.
