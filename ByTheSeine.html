By The Seine

By the Seine was painted by Vincent van Gogh in May - July 1887 with oil on canvas while he was in Paris. This artwork reflects his transition from dark, earthy tones to a lighter, more colorful palette influenced by Impressionism. Showing a peaceful riverside scene with trees and shimmering water.
