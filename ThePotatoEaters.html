The Potato Eaters

The Potato Eaters was painted with oil on canvas by Vincent van Gogh in April - May 1885 while he was in Nuenen, Netherlands. This early masterpiece depicts a peasant family gathered around a dimly lit table, sharing a simple meal of potatoes. Van Gogh used dark, earthy tones and rough brushstrokes to emphasize the harsh reality of rural life, aiming to portray the dignity and hard work of the working class.
