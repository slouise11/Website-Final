View from Vincent's Studio

View from Vincent's Studio was painted with oil on cardboard by Vincent van Gogh in June 1886 while he was living in Paris. This lesser-known work captures the urban landscape seen from his studio window, showcasing rooftops and chimneys bathed in natural light. The painting conveys a sense of quiet observation, highlighting his evolving artistic style and growing fascination with the effects of light and atmosphere on everyday scenes.
