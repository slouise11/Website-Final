Self-Portrait with Grey Felt Hat

Self-Portrait with Grey Felt Hat was painted with oil on cotton by Vincent van Gogh in September - October 1887 while he was in Paris. Using short, vibrant strokes, Van Gogh creates a halo effect around his head, contrasting cool blues and warm oranges to bring depth and intensity to his gaze.
