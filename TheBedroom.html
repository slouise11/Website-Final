The Bedroom

The Bedroom was painted with oil on canvas by Vincent van Gogh in October 1888 while he was living in the Yellow House in Arles, France. . This painting depicts his simple bedroom with bold colors and distorted perspective, creating a sense of both comfort and unease. Van Gogh used contrasting shades of blue, yellow, and red to evoke emotion rather than realism, aiming to express restfulness and stability.
