Vase of Flowers

Vase of Flowers is a still-life painting by Vincent van Gogh made with oil on canvas, created in June 1890 during his time in Auvers-sur-Oise. Reflecting his transition from darker, Dutch-inspired tones to a brighter, more colorful palette, the artwork showcases a vibrant arrangement of flowers painted with bold brushstrokes.
